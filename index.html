FAQ

Do I have to be present at the inspection?

You are welcome to attend the inspection, but it is definitely not necessary as the inspection takes anywhere from 3 to 6 hours. You will be provided a full and complete written report including photos detailing everything that is found, and are encouraged to call or email with any questions you may have.

When will I receive my report?

The completed report will be emailed to you within 24 hours of the end of the on-site property inspection.
